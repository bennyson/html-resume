About Me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Benson Saunders, a wanderer at heart, with a passion for exploration and a love for the great outdoors.

Originally hailing from the stunning landscapes of southern Utah, I've embarked on quite the journey so far. At the age of 19, I found myself immersed in the vibrant culture of Cambodia, teaching English and soaking in the rich tapestry of life in Southeast Asia.

Returning to the States, I delved into various fields, from operating CNC machinery at Seat Covers Plus to mastering the ins and outs of HVAC systems. But my thirst for adventure led me to the skies, where I pursued my dream of flying, working as a wheel chair repair supervisor at Sky Harbor Airport in Phoenix, Arizona.

After a few years of city life, I found myself drawn back to the rugged beauty of southern Utah, where I spent time at the Family Dollar Distribution Center before joining forces with my dad in Wyoming, building houses amidst the rolling hills and vast expanses.

While my career path may seem diverse, there's one constant that grounds me: my love for nature. Whether it's cultivating a thriving garden, casting a line into a tranquil fishing hole, or traversing the trails of the great outdoors, I find solace and inspiration in Mother Nature's embrace.
